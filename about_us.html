About Us

It is a non-profit organization dedicated to improving the lives of individuals and communities in need.
Established in 1999, we are committed to providing essential resources, education, healthcare, and other services to enhance the quality of life for those facing challenges.

About DonateNow

At DonateNow we believe that everyone deserves a chance to live with dignity and hope. Through the generous support of individuals and organizations, we organize campaigns and projects aimed at raising awareness and delivering aid to those suffering from poverty, illness, and social hardships.

Our Vision:


We envision a world where everyone lives with dignity, where basic needs are met, and opportunities are available for all to reach their full potential.

Our Mission:


Our mission is to focus on providing assistance to underprivileged communities, empowering individuals through education, healthcare, and social support.

Our Values:


Humanity:

Putting people’s well-being at the heart of everything we do.

Transparency:

We believe trust is the foundation of success, which is why we operate with complete openness with our donors and partners.

Collaboration:

We work together to achieve shared goals and build networks of support.

You Can Help:


You can be part of the change! Whether through donations, volunteering in our campaigns, your contribution can make a significant difference in the lives of those who need it the most.
